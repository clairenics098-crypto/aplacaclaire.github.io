Digital Ethics

Digital ethics teaches us to behave responsibly, respectfully, and honestly online. It guides how we use technology to benefit others and honor moral values.

  • Honesty: Respect intellectual property and be truthful online.
  • Respect: Avoid cyberbullying and respect privacy of others.
  • Responsible Sharing: Share helpful, positive, and verified information.
  • Faith-Guided Behavior: Use technology to inspire hope and positivity.

Examples

  • Post inspirational messages or Bible verses online responsibly.
  • Join online campaigns for community service.
  • Avoid spreading misinformation or inappropriate content.

By practicing digital ethics guided by faith, we contribute to a safer, kinder, and more positive online world.
